Math.function name-to access a math function.
Math.pow()-give the exponent of any number;
abs()-gives absolute value of any number not negative or 
positive;

round()-convert an integer number into a float number.
if the number after . is 5 or bigger then convert to the 
close bigger integer number.
ceil()-convert to the nearest bigger integer.
floor()-convert to the nearest small integer.
random()-randomized numbers
random()*6-randomized numbers within a range.
