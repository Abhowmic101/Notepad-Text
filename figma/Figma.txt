click on the lock button to lock the position of the frame.
To Hide click on the eye button.

when you remove clip content from frame the content doesn't hide
at the time you resize the frame.
when you use clip content the content get hide when you resize the frame.
selecting resize to fit option helps you to resize the frame 
so that it can fit according to the frame.

you can set the border radius from border radius option.

scale helps to resize shapes perfectly.

Y-axis means top to bottom.
X-axis means left to right.

Inspect option helps to excerpt the css codes of specific section
or part.
